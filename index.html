Rules:

  1. You earn one coin per click.

  4. Each time you buy upgrade, automatic counter goes faster.
  5. Each time you buy upgrade, the price goes up by 10 per cent.
  6. If you have less than 10 coins, upgrade button is disabled and you can't buy upgrade.
  7. Respectively, same rules are for 10x upgrade.
